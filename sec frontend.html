PGDAV College: A Legacy of Education and Excellence

Key Highlights:

Established in 1957, PGDAV College is one of the oldest colleges affiliated with the University of Delhi. It has a rich history and a commitment to providing quality education rooted in the Indian ethos.

  • Founding Vision: PGDAV College was established to address the challenges of higher education in the newly burgeoning nation. It is an integral part of the Dayanand Anglo Vedic (DAV) movement, which has been at the forefront of educational reform in India.
  • Courses Offered:
    • PGDAV offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.
    • Departments include Arts, Commerce, Computer Science, and Mathematical Sciences.
  • NAAC Accreditation: The college holds NAAC accreditation, reflecting its commitment to quality and excellence in education.
  • South Campus Affiliation: PGDAV College is located in the South Campus of the University of Delhi.
